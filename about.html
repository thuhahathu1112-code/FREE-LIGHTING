Engineering light since 2013

FREE LIGHTING is a patented LED bulb and dimmer manufacturer based in Shenzhen, China — with our own R&D, ceramic tooling and assembly under one roof.

Our Story

From a ceramic LED to 413+ models

We started in 2013 with the first miniature ceramic LED light. In 2014 we developed our patented G9 LED — engineered specifically to solve the low-lumen, color and lumen-decay problems that plagued early LED bulbs.

Since then we've grown to a 48-person team holding 88 patents, with 538+ ceramic mold types and a catalogue of 413+ LED bulb models across 25 base types — plus a full LED dimmer line introduced in 2018.
